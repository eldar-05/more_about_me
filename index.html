Favorite films

My absolute favorite film

Interstellar

Man, "Interstellar" just blows my mind every time. It's not just a sci-fi flick; it's a deep dive into humanity's future, love across dimensions, and the sheer vastness of space. The visuals are stunning, the science is intriguing (even if it makes your head spin sometimes), and the emotional core of the story about a father and daughter really hits hard. Seriously, if you haven't seen it, you're missing out on an epic journey.

A movie that always stays with me

Bridge to Terabithia

"Bridge to Terabithia" is one of those movies that, even years later, still resonates. It's a beautiful story about friendship, imagination, and finding magic in the mundane. It’s got such a raw, honest portrayal of childhood and growing up, and it's definitely a tear-jerker. It really makes you appreciate the power of connection and how important it is to have someone who understands your world, even if that world is completely made up.
